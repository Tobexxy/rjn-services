About Us At RJN Childminding Services, we believe in providing more than just childcare; we create a nurturing and loving environment where children can flourish.

Get to know us!

RJN Childminding Services was founded with the belief that every child deserves a loving and supportive environment to thrive. We are a team of passionate and experienced caregivers who are dedicated to providing high-quality childcare. With years of experience in early childhood education, we understand the importance of creating a space where children feel valued, safe, and inspired. At RJN, we emphasize individualized attention and a close-knit, family-like atmosphere. We believe in fostering strong relationships with both children and parents to ensure the best possible care. Our focus is on creating a balance between structured activities and free play, allowing children to learn, explore, and grow at their own pace.

Why Choose Us? At RJN Childminding Services, we stand out for our unwavering commitment to providing a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where your child can thrive.

Our caregivers are not only experienced and qualified but also genuinely passionate about what they do. We take the time to understand each child’s unique needs and personality, building strong, trusting relationships with both the children and their families.

We balance structured learning with creative play, offering a variety of activities that stimulate intellectual, social, and physical development.

We offer a warm and welcoming space that feels like a second home. Children are more comfortable and confident when they’re in a familiar, loving environment, and that’s exactly what we provide.

Safety is our top priority. Our facility is fully equipped with childproofing, secure entry systems, and regular safety checks to ensure that your child is always in a safe and secure space.

Our team comprises experienced and well-trained professionals dedicated to providing the highest quality care for your child. With extensive knowledge in early childhood education, we ensure that your child receives the best guidance and support.

We tailor our activities to suit the developmental stages of each child, ensuring that they are both engaging and educational. From creative arts to physical activities, our program is designed to stimulate young minds and foster a love for learning.

We believe in keeping parents informed every step of the way. Our daily progress updates ensure that you are always aware of your child’s activities, milestones, and overall development.

We understand the importance of good nutrition in a child’s growth. That’s why we offer a variety of nutritious meals and snacks, carefully planned to meet the dietary needs of young children.

We maintain a strict smoke-free and pet-free policy to ensure a clean, safe, and allergy-friendly environment for all children in our care.

Our program is packed with fun and engaging activities designed to keep your child active and entertained while fostering their creativity and social skills.

Your child will enjoy healthy and delicious meals throughout the day, including breakfast, snacks, and lunch, all prepared with their well-being in mind.
